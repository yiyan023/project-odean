Plaintiff's motion for a preliminary injunction is DENIED.

On the present record, Plaintiff has not carried its burden to show a likelihood of irreparable harm absent immediate injunctive relief. Although PX-045 reflects cohort structures and engagement metrics that may have some confidential aspects, Plaintiff's own witnesses concede that many of the underlying concepts overlap with industry discussions and its public-facing materials.

The Court is not persuaded that any threatened harm cannot be compensated through monetary damages should Plaintiff ultimately prevail at trial. The balance of equities and the public interest therefore do not favor the extraordinary remedy of a preliminary injunction at this stage.
