Defendant's motion for summary judgment is DENIED.

The parties sharply dispute whether Defendant independently developed its recommendation models or instead built them using Plaintiff's cohort structures and internal metrics. PX-045 and related communications create a triable issue as to the extent of overlap between the two systems and the source of that overlap.

Because material factual disputes remain regarding access, similarity, and independent development, the Court cannot resolve liability as a matter of law. Those issues will be submitted to the jury.
