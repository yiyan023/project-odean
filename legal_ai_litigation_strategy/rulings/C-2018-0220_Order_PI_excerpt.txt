Plaintiff's motion for a preliminary injunction is DENIED.

At this early stage, Plaintiff has not shown a likelihood of irreparable harm absent an injunction barring Defendant's ad platform from participating in real-time bidding. The record suggests substantial convergence in industry practices, and Plaintiff has not yet isolated any concrete, non-public component of its bidding stack that would support such broad relief.

Any economic injury arising from alleged misappropriation can, on the present showing, be addressed through monetary damages if Plaintiff ultimately prevails. The extraordinary remedy of a preliminary injunction is therefore unwarranted.
