Defendant's Daubert motion to exclude the opinions of Plaintiff's damages expert, Dr. Naomi Keller, is GRANTED IN PART and DENIED IN PART.

Dr. Keller's "blockbuster" scenario—in which Plaintiff purportedly loses hundreds of millions in profits based on a chain of speculative assumptions about future approvals and market dominance—is not grounded in the factual record and will be excluded. The methodology underlying that scenario is insufficiently reliable under Rule 702.

However, Dr. Keller also offers a more conservative lost-profits analysis tied to documented deal flow and concrete opportunities that Plaintiff contends were compromised by the alleged misappropriation. That limited opinion, which relies on contemporaneous forecasts and executed term sheets, is sufficiently tethered to the evidence to be presented to the jury, subject to vigorous cross-examination.
